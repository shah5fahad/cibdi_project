Abstract

The Internet of Things (IoT) represents a technological revolution that enables physical devices to communicate and interact over the internet.

This paper explores the architecture, applications, advantages, challenges, and future potential of IoT.

With the increasing integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI), 5G, and edge computing, IoT is transforming industries, cities, and human life.

The research also highlights security issues and the need for global standards for successful IoT adoption.
